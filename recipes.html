1. Basic sponge cake

Ingredients

  • 4 Egg yolks
  • 45 ml Milk
  • 40 g Melted butter
  • 4 Egg white
  • 100 g Sugar
  • 110 g Cake flour
  • 1/2 tbsp Baking powder

Instructions

  1. Put the egg yolk in the bowl and let loose, then add milk and melted butter and mix.
  2. Put egg whites in another bowl and whip it. When big bubble comes up, divide sugar into three and whip to make meringue.
  3. Add sitfed dry ingredients and mix.
  4. Add egg yolk mixture and mix.
  5. Pour the dough into the pan and bake in a 180 degree preheated oven for 35~40 minutes.

2. Souffle pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg separated
  • 1 tbsp Cake flour
  • 2 tsp Cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp Sugar

Instructions

  1. In small a bowl with egg yolk, add flour, cornstarch, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix well and set aside.
  2. In a larger bowl with the egg white, mix until foamy then gradually add sugar. Whisk until you get glossy stiff peaks.
  3. Add half of the egg yolk mixture to the egg white and mix well. Make sure it’s thoroughly mixed.
  4. Add the rest of the yolk mixture and gently fold it in - don’t overmix at this point.
  5. In a pan on medium-low heat, wipe some oil on it then layer scoops of the batter on the pan. Add two tsps of water and cover for 2 minutes.
  6. To make it fluffy, add an extra scoop of batter on top of and close the lid for another minute. Flip or roll the pancake over and add two more tsps of water.
  7. Cover and cook for another 3-4 minutes until the exterior of the pancake looks dry. Make sure to adjust the heat if pan is getting too hot.
  8. Let it sit after removing the lid for another two minutes to set and transfer to plate. Dust with powdered sugar and enjoy!

3. Cupcake

Ingredients

  • 5 Eggs
  • 100 g Cake flour
  • 60 g Corn oil
  • 60 g Milk
  • 70 g Sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix cake flour and corn oil together in a mixing bowl.
  2. Separate egg yolks from egg whites. We need 5 large eggs.
  3. Add egg yolks, and milk into the mixing bowl using a low speed until combined.
  4. Beat egg whites until it becomes firm peaks. Add sugar 3 times during the beating.
  5. Mix some egg whites with batter in the mixing bowl, then mix the mixture with the remaining egg whites.
  6. Fill each cupcake 2/3 full.
  7. Pre-heat oven and bake in 160°C/325°F for 25-30 minutes.

4. Milk tea

Ingredients

  • 500 g Milk
  • 10 g Black tea
  • 15 g Brown Sugar

Instructions

  1. Place milk in a pot and simmer on low heat until edges bubble.
  2. When the bubbles rise, add the tea and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add brown sugar and stir, then turn the heat off and lower it.
  4. Strain the tea through a sieve and put it in a glass jar and store it in the refrigerator.
  5. Enjoy the cool milk tea.

5. Strawberry milk

Ingredients

  • 12 Strawberries
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1 tbs Sugar

Instructions

  1. Clean and cut off stems of strawberries.
  2. Use a fork or potato masher and mash strawberries until squashed to your liking.
  3. Add sugar and let it sit until dissolved around 5 min.
  4. Dice the left over strawberries finely.
  5. To serve, pour the strawberry sauce (mashed strawberries), diced strawberries, and milk and mix and drink.
  6. If you want to store, sterilize a glass jar and pour into it like step 5 and shake well before serving.

6. Dalgona coffee

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Instant coffee
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Hot water
  • 300 ml Milk

Instructions

  1. Put instant coffee and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Adding hot water and whip it with a hand mixer on a high speed for 5 min.
  3. Pour some milk in a cup and put the completed cream on top.
  4. Mix the cream and milk well and enjoy.

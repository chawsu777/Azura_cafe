Sparkling Rose Lemonade
Sparkling rose lemonade is a refreshing rose cocktail.
This drink is sweet, tart and spicy - the rim of the glass is coated
with a mix of sugar and lemon zest. It is made with rose syrup,
lemon juce, sparkling water and square ice cubes.
It’s really delicious, and floral.
